# Enchated Blade of Elemental Tempest
 
 A sword with swirling elemental patterns etched onto its blade. The patterns come to life during the ability's activation, creating a dazzling display of elemental energy.
 
 Damage: 110% of weapon damage

 Cooldown: 5 seconds

 # Fury of the Ancient Titan

 The weapon itself will undergo a metamorphosis during the channeling process. The hilt transforms into a miniature titan, with the blade becoming a swirling vortex of elemental energy

 Cooldwon: 15 seconds

 # The Celestial Blessing of the Divine Warrior

 The weapon itself transforms into a magnificent, glowing blade, adorned with intricate celestial patterns. These patterns come to life during the ability's activation, creating a mesmerizing display of divine energy.

 Cooldown:  30 seconds

 # Dragon's Fury: Rage of the First One

The weapon transforms into a fearsome dragon's claw, engulfed in flames. The player's aura mimics the scorching breath of a dragon, creating an awe-inspiring display of fire and destruction.

Cooldown: 60 seconds
